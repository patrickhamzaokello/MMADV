ABOUT US

Who We Are

Mulima Mountain Adventures (MMADV) was founded by Job Soyekwo in April of 2013. MMADV headquarters are in Sipi on the beautiful mountain side of Mt. Elgon in Eastern Uganda. Sipi is 70 km from Mbale, 21 km from Kapchorwa and 235 km from Kampala. Mulima mountain adventures started in 2003 where we were able to make a proposal to north Alpines club in Italy of which they sent three trainees to train us for short time like one year.

Why Choose Us

Our staff makes your safety their highest priority. We have a well-trained staff that has many systems in place to keep you safe and high quality safety gear. All of our guides have been trained and certified by Club Alpino Italiano. Abseiling ropes: 120mm static ropes from both Europe and USA Rock Climbing ropes: 10mm and 11.5mm thick 80m dynamic ropes. MMADV also uses seat belts, express carabiners, screw clubs, ATC, figure eight, gloves and helmets.

SERVICES WE OFFER


Amazing Rock Climbing

We offer a lot of exhilarating rock climbing with mulima mountain adventures with around 13boltd routes with height of around 45meters in far East in mountain Elgon region in sipifalls which can challenge and invigorate you're mind.


Abseiling

Adventure lovers looking for a rush of adrenaline on their visit can abseil/rappel from the top of the 100metre high Sipi Falls, or try climbing on the numerous high cliffs. The close proximity of the falling water and panoramic views provide a once in a lifetime experience. Mulima mountain adventures offers individualised and bespoke multi-day summit treks for those interested in exploring the mountain.


Cultural Tourism

Kapchorwa district is fast becoming a world class running destination for elite athletes and amateurs alike.Run the local trails or choose to join the local athletes during their training schedule.Our connection with a local NGO allows a variety of options that can be easily tailored to suit your interests.


Coffee Tours

Trace the story of coffee from seed to cup. Sipi Falls Arabica Coffee is some of the finest in the world. Meet local farmers to tour their plantation and learn about the lifecycle of the crop and their role in the process. Visit and tour the local processing plant to see the selected green beans before shipment. Finally learn the traditional art of roasting your own coffee and enjoy your own cup.A percentage of proceeds goes towards locally driven initiatives. Mt. Elgon is known for growing some of the best coffee in all of Uganda. Coffee is Ugandaâ€™s number one export


Waterfalls Tours

Walking tours near the falls offer spectacular views and the ability to get very close to the falls, even behind some of them. We offer swimming opportunities at the bottom of the falls if you are willing to brave the cold water. 15 dollars for basically 3-4 hour's.


Hiking

Winding trails lead hikers through local communities to roaring waterfalls, lush farms and forests, and magnificent views of the Karamoja plains. Sipi Falls is a hiker's paradise waiting to be explored. Our guides are friendly, knowledgable, and professional. Use of guides accredited with the Sipi Falls Tour Guides Association helps to overcome challenges in the community with school drop-out rates and unprofessional guiding practices.
